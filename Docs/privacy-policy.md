# Privacy Policy for Soccerholic

**Last updated:** May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Soccerholic ("the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App.

## Information We Collect

### 1) Account Information (if you sign in)
If you create an account or sign in, we may collect:
- Email address
- Username or display name
- User ID associated with your account

### 2) Gameplay and App Data
We may collect data needed to operate core features, such as:
- Game results (scores, correct and incorrect answers, round progress)
- Game session events (for example, game start and game completion)
- Multiplayer room data (room codes, player readiness, and scores) when you use online multiplayer features

### 3) User-Submitted Reports (Flags)
If you flag a question or player, we may collect:
- Question identifier, question text, and answer options
- Selected/correct answer index metadata
- Your user ID and display name to review reported content

### 4) Notifications (optional)
If you enable notifications, the App may process:
- Device push token used to deliver push notifications

### 5) Photos and Media
The App does not access your personal photo library. Player images shown in the App come from bundled assets and/or remote content sources used by the App.

## How We Use Information
We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain single-player and multiplayer functionality
- Create and manage user accounts and sign-in
- Save and display scores, progress, and multiplayer state
- Review flagged content and improve question quality
- Troubleshoot issues and improve app performance

## How We Share Information
We do not sell personal information.

We may share information in these cases:
- Service providers: We use backend providers (such as Firebase) to support authentication, database storage, and multiplayer sync.
- Legal/safety: If required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect users and the App.

## Data Storage and Security
Data may be stored on secure servers operated by our service providers. We apply reasonable safeguards, but no method of transmission or storage is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

## Data Retention
We retain data only as long as needed to provide app functionality and legitimate business purposes (such as security and abuse prevention), unless a longer period is required by law.

## Your Choices
Depending on app functionality in your build:
- You can disable notifications in your device settings.
- You can request account/data deletion by contacting us.

## Children's Privacy
The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate deletion steps.

## International Users
Your information may be processed in countries other than your own where the App or its service providers operate.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date when changes are made.
